LunchMeet support

Help for the LunchMeet iOS app — plan lunches, meet people, and chat with your group.

Contact us

Email: jpmitchell.grad@hotmail.com

We try to reply within a few business days. Include your device model and iOS version if you’re reporting a bug.

Common questions

  • Sign-in issues: Use the same method you used to create your account (e.g. Apple). If you’re stuck, email us from the address on your account if possible.
  • Location: LunchMeet uses your location to show nearby restaurants and maps. You can change this anytime in Settings → LunchMeet → Location.
  • Safety / reporting: Use in-app reporting and blocking tools. For urgent safety concerns, contact local authorities.
