Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026. This policy describes how LunchMeet (“we”, “the app”) handles information when you use our iOS application.

Account and authentication

We use Supabase for account services. You may sign in with Apple, email and password, or other methods we offer. Authentication data is processed according to the method you choose and our backend configuration.

Profile and photos

We store profile information you provide (for example, display name) and profile photos you choose from your photo library. The app is configured not to request camera access for image picking.

Location

With your permission, we use your device location while you use the app to show maps, nearby restaurants, and lunch-related features. You can change location access in iOS Settings for LunchMeet.

Messaging

Group chat messages for lunches are delivered through our backend (including realtime features where enabled). Do not share sensitive personal data you are not comfortable having processed for this purpose.

Ratings and safety

Optional ratings after meetups and user reports may be stored to improve trust and safety. Use in-app tools responsibly.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may send you alerts related to lunches and account activity according to your device and in-app settings.
